In this documentation you will find information about Doctor Assistant AI's APIs and web-components,

## What is Doctor Assistant AI?

Doctor Assistant AI is an artificial intelligence platform that provides technology solutions for doctors and patients. 
As a B2B2C SaaS, Doctor Assistant AI offers a variety of AI solutions to serve your clients in a 
personalized and efficient way, without much bureaucracy and complex implementation.

## How can I start using Doctor Assistant AI?

To start using Doctor Assistant AI, you can request your registration on the website by filling out the contact form 
[here](https://www.doctorassistant.ai/prontuario/#contato).

If you already have an account, continue with the documentation to understand how to use Doctor Assistant AI's 
APIs and web-components.
